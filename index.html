IntegrityCoinAI Whitepaper

1. Introduction

Corruption is a serious threat in both the real world and the blockchain world. From the misuse of public funds to liquidity pool manipulation and rug pulls, many parties are harmed. INTEGRITYCOINAI (IGTCOIN_AI) comes as a blockchain and AI-based solution to create a transparent, fair, and manipulation-free ecosystem.

By using the Solana Network, INTEGRITYCOINAI offers fast transactions, low fees, and a secure ecosystem. This token aims not only to combat corruption in the real world but also to protect investors from financial crimes in the blockchain.

2. Technology & Advantages

Transparent Blockchain

All transactions can be publicly audited without manipulation.

AI for Corruption & Manipulation Detection

AI will analyze transactions to detect corruption patterns and potential rug pulls.

Secure Smart Contracts

Smart contracts are verified to avoid exploitation.

Rewards for Whistleblowers

Incentives for those who report corruption and fraud in both the real world and crypto.

Investor Protection

AI systems provide early warnings for high-risk projects.
